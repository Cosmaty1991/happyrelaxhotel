              Restaurant

              Fresh and seasonal british fare

              Happy Relax Restaurant celebrates the Great British kitchen showcasing the breadth of Britain’s culinary strengths. The menu focuses on seasonality, sustainability, and traceability through close relationships with artisan producers.

              The breathtaking dining room sees plush burnt orange leather offset by glittering mirrored panels and expansive picture windows, whilst two private dining areas offer privacy for special celebrations and corporate events.

              Happy Relax Bar, featuring a marble island, offers the epitome of chic socialising in London. It's the ideal place to meet for a glass of Champagne, wine or beer, to sip an aperitif or classic cocktail, and it offers an all-day light lunch and dinner menu for the casual diner.

              About

              Happy Relax Hotel offers the perfect setting for rare moments of romance, adventure, and excitement

              Happy Relax Hotel is an established and well known hotel in the heart of London. The hotel has a good reputation due to the outstanding customer service, great location and luxurious rooms.

              Our guests are well received by our multi-lingual employees. We strive to make your stay at Happy Relax Hotel a memorable and an enjoyable one.

              Happy Relax Hotel is a beautiful hotel, located in a quaint little street close to the citycenter.

              The rooms at Happy Relax Hotel are comfortable and clean. In addition, the rooms have all the standard amenities to enhance your "home-away-from-home" feeling.

              There are several options when it comes to booking a room at Happy Relax Hotel. Feel free to browse the website for the perfect option that suits you and your family.

              We recommend that you view some of the reviews our guests have written about us, on Tripadvisor and Facebook.

              The employees of Happy Relax Hotel look forward to meeting you and welcome you to our hotel, and we hope you have an enjoyable and memorable stay in London. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us.

              History

              Happy Relax Hotel was built in 1997 by Tom Smith in the center of London.

              For twenty years Happy Relax Hotel has undergone many changes and soon became the place for relax for many tourists.
